## Welcome to Enishiya

Tucked away on the second floor of a quiet building in Susukino, Enishiya is a hideaway izakaya (Japanese gastropub) beloved by locals. Our chef personally selects the freshest seasonal ingredients each morning. The warm wooden interior, intimate counter seating, and private rooms create the perfect setting for an authentic Hokkaido dining experience.

**Selected as one of Japan's Top 100 Izakayas (Tabelog 2024)**

## 🍶 DRINKS | お飲み物

### Japanese Sake (Nihonshu)
Our specialty. We offer a rotating selection of premium sake from breweries across Japan, chosen to pair with our seasonal dishes.

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Junmai Daiginjo** | The finest grade — floral, fruity, elegant |
| **Junmai Ginjo** | Aromatic with a clean finish |
| **Junmai** | Full-bodied, rich — pairs well with bold flavors |
| **Honjozo** | Light and dry — easy to drink |

> 💡 *Not sure what to order? Ask for "Omakase" (chef's choice) — we'll match your sake to your food.*

**Sake serving styles:**
- **Cold (Reishu)** — refreshing, highlights aroma
- **Room temperature (Hiya)** — balanced flavor
- **Warm (Nurukan)** — gentle warmth, smooth
- **Hot (Atsukan)** — bold and comforting

### Other Drinks
Beer, Shochu (distilled spirit), Highball, Wine, Whisky, Soft Drinks

## ALLERGY INFORMATION | アレルギー情報

Please inform your server of any food allergies or dietary restrictions **before ordering**.

Common allergens in our dishes:
- 🦀 Crustaceans (crab, shrimp)
- 🐟 Fish / Raw fish
- 🥚 Egg (in yukhoe / tartare dishes)
- 🫘 Soy (soy sauce)
- 🌾 Wheat (tempura, fried dishes)

> We are happy to accommodate your needs where possible.
> 「アレルギーがあります」= "I have an allergy"
